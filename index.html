Haarlem

Haarlem , is a city located in the province of North Holland,Netherlands and is also it's capital. It is known for its Hofjes (enclosed courtyard gardens). These unique 21 courtyards surrounded by almshouses (charitable housing) hark back to a bygone era when wealthy members of society founded them to house poor, unmarried or widowed women.The entrance to the courtyard was often richly decorated and locked at night. spanning from the ancient (the oldest was founded in 1395).

The oldest and most modern hofjes in the country are located next to each other in the centre of Haarlem. The oldest remaining example is the Hofje van Bakenes, founded in 1395 by Dirc van Bakenes. The Johan Enschedéhofje is the latest addition, completed in 2007 to a design by Joost Swarte and Henk Döll. This hofje is named after the Johan Enschedé printing company which once stood here.Most of the city’s hofjes are free to visit between 10:00 and 17:00.

Haarlem is a definite must-see if you want to get a truly local experience in Holland.
